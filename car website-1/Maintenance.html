    Quality Control Center

    Ford Mustang INVENTORY

    Before any classic is marketed through our Ford Mustang Charlotte showroom, it receives a comprehensive, Multi-Point Vehicle Inspection. Performed by one of our ASE Certified Master Technicians, the Multi-Point Vehicle Inspection (LINK) focuses on the car’s drivability, suspension, brakes, fuel system, electric system, exhaust system, exterior and interior. As a dedicated group of enthusiasts, our goal is to help our customers make informed decisions on whatever car they desire. As an organization, we have the talent and ability to identify potential mechanical issues and, if the purchaser sees fit, remedy those issues and solidify their investment.



    CONSIGNMENTS

    At the Ford Mustang Performance Center, world-class service extends to the cars we own and the cars we represent for discerning sellers. As reflected in our 90% sale-through rate, RK Motors Charlotte has perfected a marketing strategy that bridges the gap between high-end buyers and high-end sellers. The consignment process begins with our Multi-Point Vehicle Inspection, which ensures the car is accurately represented and optimally priced. Our goal is simple: offer buyers the best classics on the market while earning consignors top return on their investment.



    AFTER-SALES SERVICE AND CUSTOMIZATION

    Ever find a choice classic that you fall in love with, but then pass on because it isn’t ideal? Maybe you wanted a car with air conditioning, or maybe you wanted something with an automatic transmission. Well, the beauty of having a dealership that’s backed by a full-service facility is ease of modification. The Ford Mustang Performance Center will be happy to talk to you about modifying any purchased inventory car to best suit your needs. Remember, one of the greatest aspects of the collector car hobby is customization.
